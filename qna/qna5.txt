What does the SVG viewbox represent?
A piece of graph paper that can be shrunk or expanded while maintaining the same plotting points

What happens to SVG elements positioned outside the viewbox?
They are clipped and not displayed, but still exist within the SVG structure

How can SVG viewbox be used for animation or revealing elements?
By moving elements outside the viewbox and then bringing them into view, creating a clipping effect

How does SVG viewbox differ from image cropping in a JPEG?
In SVG, elements outside the viewbox are still present in the file, whereas in JPEG they are permanently removed

What potential issue can occur when designers export SVG files?
They might accidentally include additional unintended elements from outside the main artboard
